# Scroll

Use these terms to describe motion tied to scrolling, viewport entry, or route changes.

- **Scroll reveal**: Elements animate into place as they enter the viewport.
- **Scroll-driven animation**: Animation progress is directly controlled by scroll position.
- **Parallax**: Foreground and background layers move at different speeds to create depth.
- **Page transition**: Motion that plays when moving between pages or routes.
- **View transition**: A browser-supported transition that visually connects two document or UI states, often with shared elements.
